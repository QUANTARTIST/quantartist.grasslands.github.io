EXPLORE Life Forms of the Savanna Grassland Ecosystems


Use your mouse to explore the relationships between different plant and animal life forms found in the Savanna Grassland ecosystem. Click on "Life Form Species" to group life forms into their respective families. Click on "Expand Network Relationships" to see how species are connected. Connecting nodes may occur for many reasons; species are part of the same family, species prey on one another or depend on eachother within an ecosystem for survival. Mouse over the colored nodes to view the names of the different species in English. Each species family is represented by a color.

Note that, in this instance, plants have not been grouped in sub-species. Species reflected can be found in different parts of the world.
